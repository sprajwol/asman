Association of St. Mary's Alumnae Nepal

Establish in 1998 AD, Asman Nepal is a national level non-governmental organization (NGO), dedicated fully towards the better of children and women with fulfillment of their needs, protection of their rights and promotion of their growth and independence. With strong advocation of child rights, we believe that to become aware people need to hear and to make people hear we need to speak. Our voices may ring in ears today but tomorrow it will enlighten towards a humane society with gleeful children and women led society towards prosperity and growth.

It also conducts awareness oriented programs related to health and environment.
